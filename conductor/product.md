# Product Guide

## Overview
Barcode Stock Control is an Android application designed to provide a rapid, offline-first barcode scanning experience for inventory management, specifically tailored for pharmacy staff working in fast-paced or low-connectivity environments.

## Target Audience
- **Primary Users:** Pharmacy Staff.
- **Environment:** Aisles and storage areas where fast scanning of medication boxes is essential.

## Core Value Proposition
- **High-Speed Entry:** Leverages Google ML Kit and a streamlined grid UI to maximize the speed of data entry.
- **Simple Integration:** Offers frictionless data transfer via simple text/CSV export, ensuring easy ingestion by legacy pharmacy or inventory systems.

## Product Scope and Expandability
- **Strictly Single-Purpose:** The app is deliberately kept lean and focused on its core offline workflow. It is not intended to grow into a complex, cloud-synced inventory management system.
- **Future Considerations:**
  - **Basic Analytics:** Future updates may introduce session statistics and scanning metrics.
  - **Enhanced Export:** Potential integrations with platforms like Google Drive, Email, or Slack to streamline data handoff.

## Architecture Alignment
The product goals emphasize speed, reliability (100% offline), and simplicity, aligning with the planned modern Android architecture (Jetpack Compose, Clean Architecture) and ML Kit integration.
